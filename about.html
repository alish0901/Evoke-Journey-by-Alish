About Evoke Journeys

Our story, mission, and the passion behind our travel services

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Bibek Shrestha, Evoke Journeys began with a simple idea: to create travel experiences that go beyond the ordinary. What started as a passion project has grown into a respected travel agency known for its personalized service and attention to detail.

Bibek's extensive travel experience across Asia, Europe, and North America forms the foundation of our company's expertise. We understand what makes a trip memorable because we've experienced it ourselves.

Our Mission

At Evoke Journeys, we believe travel should be transformative. Our mission is to evoke emotions, inspire discovery, and create journeys that stay with you long after you return home.

We're committed to sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities while providing our clients with authentic cultural experiences.

Meet Our Founder

Bibek Shrestha

Founder & CEO

With 4 years of travel experience across five continents, Bibek brings unparalleled expertise to every journey he plans. His personal philosophy is that travel should challenge, inspire, and transform.

As the sole team member, Bibek handles every aspect of your travel planning personally, ensuring consistency and attention to detail from initial consultation to your return home.
